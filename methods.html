Assignment 1 - 1.2 Methods,Skills and Resources for Digital Projects

IT Project management is essential to deliver successful projects. Trello is a very useful tool to plan and monitor projects throughout their life cycle. It assists with visibility for all members of a project team. Effeciency, productivity and stability are improved by incorporating an industry standard structured approach to development. Team capacity and customer demand can be managed and aligned more quickly with tools such as Trello.

Relevant development skills will be required to make projects succeed. According to Forbes, the most in demand languages are:-

  1. Python
  2. Java
  3. Javascript
  4. C#
  5. PHP

Slack is the communication tool of preference for the IT industry. Slack relies on channels and DM's as the two main methods of communications. Channels allow posts to be hash tagged so that they are organised in a way that allows users to filter in relevant posts. DM's or Direct Messages allow for users to connect on an individual basis.
